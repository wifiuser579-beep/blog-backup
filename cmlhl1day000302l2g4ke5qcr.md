Even with branded links, execution matters.

### Mistake 1: Using Vague Slugs

* Bad: `yourbrand.co/link123`
    
* Good: `yourbrand.co/free-guide`

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **1\. How much does a branded short link improve CTR compared to generic shorteners?**

Branded short links typically improve CTR by 25-40% depending on the channel. Email and social media see the highest gains because visual trust matters most in those environments.

### **2\. Do I need technical skills to set up branded short links?**

No technical skills required. You add a domain to your URL shortener platform, copy two DNS records to your domain provider, and verification completes in 10-15 minutes. Most platforms provide step-by-step guidance.

### **3\. Can I use my main website domain for branded short links?**

Yes, you can use your main domain or a dedicated short domain. Many brands use their main domain for consistency or buy a short branded domain specifically for links. Both approaches work well.

### **4\. Will branded short links affect my website SEO?**

Branded short links use 301 redirects which pass link equity. Your SEO is not negatively impacted. Some brands see positive signals from increased branded search queries after deploying branded links across channels.

### **5\. How do I track which channel drives the best results with branded links?**

Create unique branded links for each channel and campaign. Your URL shortener analytics dashboard shows clicks, locations, devices, and conversions by link. This gives you clear attribution without complex UTM setups.
