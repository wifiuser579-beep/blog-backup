## Feature-by-Feature Breakdown

### 1\. Custom Domains and Branded Links

* **SimpleURL:** [Pro plan includes 1 custom domain, and the Team plan at $15.99/month supports up to 3 domains](https://www.simpleurl.tech/pricing). The platform provides clear, step-by-step guidance tailored to small business owners.
    
* **Bitly:** Custom domain support starts with the Growth plan at $29/month; the Core plan at $10/month does not include custom domains. You can connect domains like yourbrand.link or [go.yourbrand.com](http://go.yourbrand.com) once DNS is configured.
    
* **TinyURL:** Pro plan at $13/month includes up to 3 branded domains, which is generous for freelancers or users managing multiple brands. The setup flow is designed to be beginner-friendly.
    

> **Winner:** TinyURL for domain quantity (3 domains on a single plan). Bitly Growth, SimpleURL Pro, and SimpleURL Team are all strong options for single-brand setups.

### 2\. Link Analytics and Tracking

* **SimpleURL Pro:** Delivers a full analytics dashboard with unlimited history, including total and unique clicks, time-based charts, geographic breakdown, device types, languages, and browser/OS data, along with a [built-in UTM parameter](https://www.simpleurl.tech/utm-builder) workflow.

* **TinyURL Pro:** Provides advanced click analytics with unlimited trackable clicks, including geographic and device data; the exact analytics data retention period is not clearly stated on the pricing page.
    

> **Winner:** Bitly Growth for mature enterprise reporting, **SimpleURL Pro for best value with unlimited data history**, and TinyURL Pro as a capable middle option.

### 3\. Team Collaboration

* **SimpleURL:** Pro plan is single-user, while the Team plan at $15.99/month supports up to 5 users with shared access to links, domains, and analytics.
    
* **Bitly:** Core and Growth plans are single-user only. True team collaboration begins at the Premium tier ($199/month) for up to 5 users, which is a steep jump for many small businesses.
    
* **TinyURL:** Pro plan is built for individual users and does not advertise multi-user or team management features.
    

> **Winner:** SimpleURL Team plan. Getting 5-seat collaboration at $15.99/month compared to Bitly Premium at $199/month is outstanding value for small teams.
