## What Are Branded Links?

Branded links are custom short URLs featuring your brand’s domain instead of shared domains like SimpleURL or [bit.ly](http://bit.ly).

**For example, rather than “**[**smplu.link/z3x92,”**](http://bit.ly/z3x92,%E2%80%9D) **you might have “**[**go.yourbrand.com/sale”**](http://go.yourbrand.com/sale.%E2%80%9D)

These links foster brand recognition and trust, encourage clicks, and enable robust tracking. Platforms like [SimpleURL](https://www.simpleurl.tech/) enable marketers small teams and agencies to easily generate, manage, and analyze branded links.

## The Psychology Behind Branded Links’ Effectiveness

Branded links trigger subconscious trust signals. Users view them as professional and secure before even opening content. This lessens cognitive friction and increases spontaneous clicks.

E-commerce companies use branded links in social bios and emails to drive sales, while agencies rely on them for polished client communications.

## Creating Branded Links: Step-by-Step Guide

### **1\. Choose Your Branded Domain**

Select an easy-to-remember subdomain like “[go.yourbrand.com](http://go.yourbrand.com)” to strengthen brand coherence.

### **2\. Pick a URL Shortener**

Use platforms like SimpleURL that support custom domains, analytics, UTM tracking, and team collaboration.

### **3\. Configure DNS and Security**

Set up CNAME records pointing to your shortening service. Enable automated SSL certificates for HTTPS links, ensuring user trust.

### **4\. Create Campaign Links**

Design descriptive slugs (e.g., [yourbrand.com/summer-sale](http://yourbrand.com/summer-sale)). Always apply UTM parameters for detailed performance attribution. Test across devices before launch.

### Call to Action (CTA) Best Practices for Branded Links

Enhance CTR by pairing branded links with compelling CTAs:

* Use urgency (e.g., [yourbrand.com/limited-time](http://yourbrand.com/limited-time))
    
* Highlight benefits (e.g., [yourbrand.com/free-guide](http://yourbrand.com/free-guide))
    
* Personalize slugs where possible (e.g., [yourbrand.com/jane-special](http://yourbrand.com/jane-special))
    

Well-crafted CTAs combined with branded links drive higher conversion rates.
