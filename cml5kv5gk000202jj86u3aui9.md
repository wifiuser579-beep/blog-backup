## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q1: What is the average CTR increase from using branded short links in e-commerce?**

Branded short links typically increase click-through rates by 30-39% compared to generic URL shorteners. This improvement comes from enhanced trust and professional appearance that reduces spam perception.

### **Q2: Can Branded Short Links Be Tracked in Shopify and WooCommerce?**

Yes, quality short link platforms integrate seamlessly with Shopify and WooCommerce through UTM parameters and tracking pixels. You can track complete customer journeys from link click to purchase completion.

### **Q3: Should I use different branded short links for email versus SMS campaigns?**

Absolutely. Use unique short links for each marketing channel to track performance accurately. This attribution data shows which channels deliver the best ROI, allowing optimized budget allocation.

### **Q4: How long does it take to set up branded short links for my store?**

Initial setup takes 1-2 hours including custom domain connection and analytics integration. Once configured, creating new branded links for campaigns takes just seconds through dashboard or API.

### **Q5: Is It Possible to Update a Short Link’s Destination After Publishing?**

Premium URL shortening platforms allow destination URL editing without changing the published short link. This feature enables campaign flexibility and fixes broken links without reprinting materials or resending messages
