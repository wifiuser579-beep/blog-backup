## **What Is Affiliate Link Shortening and How Does It Work?**

![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/uploads/covers/682870ca34178af533acf2f9/605e2407-e7c7-4521-990a-e3792fb4615f.png align="center")

Affiliate link shortening converts your long tracking URL into a short, clean, shareable link that redirects the user to the same destination.

**The process is simple:**

1.  You paste your raw affiliate URL into an affiliate link shortener.
    
2.  The tool generates a short URL (for example: `go.yourbrand.com/airpods`).
    
3.  When someone clicks, they pass through the redirect layer invisibly and land on the affiliate page.
    
4.  The click, location, device, and referrer get recorded in your analytics dashboard.

## **Affiliate Link Cloaking vs Link Shortening: What Is the Difference?**

**These two terms often get confused. They solve different problems.**

1.  **Link shortening** converts a long URL to a short one for cleaner sharing. The primary benefit is visual cleanliness and branding.
    
2.  **Affiliate link cloaking** hides the affiliate tracking parameters from the visible URL.
    
3.  The goal is to prevent commission hijacking and look less promotional.
    

Both approaches serve the same audience goal: make the link trustworthy and shareable.

**A branded short link accomplishes both simultaneously because:**

*   It hides the raw tracking parameters
    
*   It shows your brand domain instead
    
*   It records analytics on every click
    

> **Pro Tip: Check your affiliate program's terms before cloaking. Some networks like Amazon Associates have specific rules about masking referral links. Always verify compliance before deploying.**
